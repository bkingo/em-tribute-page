Bringing Back the Future

"It's very difficult to start companies and quite painful, and that's important to bear in mind. That's probably not encouraging. Let me put it this way: If you need inspiring words, dont do it." - Elon Musk on TV

Elon Musk is a visionary that has been able to repeatedly [see an unseen need for something, and then take advantage of that void.] *change this* First it was launching Zip2 and its online city guide software, and later co-founding Paypal.com (formerly X.com), which helped usher in the era of online banking.

Despite his early sucesses with the Internet, he has since moved on. While many of this generation's smartest minds spend time getting people to click on ads, Musk instead is creating new companies in various other industries, and although he appears a newcomer, these businesses are thriving today.

As different as his companies may be, they are all united by an underlying drive to usher humanity along to advancement and sustainability. Given Musk's net worth at $13.9 billion, it might seem strange that some of his major projects are so successful yet still have strong conservationist intentions.

Rather than coincidence, it is more likely that Musk has a knack for spotting the interesection between design, technology, and business, allowing him to take profitable risks while still achieving humanitarian aims. As a result, quite a few people are starting to turn their heads.
